INTRODUCING DOTA 2

This is published by Valve Gaming and created by IceFrog and was officially released on Steam for Microsoft Windows on July 9, 2013. DOTA 2 or the Defence of the Ancient is from its earlier game called World of Warcraft: Frozen Throne and now update in the new and more updated graphics and gameplays. There are 115 hero you can choose to play with from steampunks, pirates, sorcerers, sea creatures, gods, knights, and even the undead. There are 10 players in a game 5 each team and have to choose different character to play with, each character is called hero and have different unique abilities and gameplay. To make your hero more powerful, you need to buy different type of item which have different power to use and to buy items you need to gain coins to do that: you need to kill a enemy hero to gain coins you can gain coins in killing creeps that located in different spot of the lane especially in the three main lane. If an enemy killed you, some of your coins will be taken. To win and the main goal is to destroy there ancient there main building but also take care of your ancient, they may destroy your ancient too. And if you have the skill and the power to cary your team they may commend your bravey afterwards.
